    • BCDF GREEN



    BCDF is committed to increasing awareness of environmental issues and reducing its own ecological impact. Our New York facilities are powered in large part by solar panels, and we maintain an organic garden (and adjacent compost pile) to provide our employees with produce. Additionally, we work to develop our on-site resources in a manner that preserves the natural beauty and ecological health of the region.

    We believe that staying local is a great way to stay green. We house our crews in locally owned apartments and hotels, use regionally produced food for our catering, and shop at locally owned stores.
