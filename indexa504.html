    • FILMS


    BCDF strives to tell the kind of story movie lovers long to see—films that celebrate the human adventure, with its humor and passion, conflict and connection, and transformative moments of discovery.

    Our projects have included Oscar-winning and Oscar-nominated talent (e.g. Al Pacino, Jesse Eisenberg, Jane Fonda, Vera Farmiga, Catherine Keener, Melissa Leo) and other award-winning actors such as Kirsten Dunst, Elizabeth Olsen, and Zac Efron.

    BCDF’s first movie, Higher Ground, premiered at the 2011 Sundance Film Festival. BCDF’s second movie, Peace, Love, & Misunderstanding, premiered at the 2011 Toronto International Film Festival. BCDF has premiered three more films at Sundance—Bachelorette, Liberal Arts, and Why Stop Now.

    BCDF’s most recent projects include The Pirates of Somalia (Evan Peters, Al Pacino) and the upcoming Departures (Asa Butterfield, Maisie Williams, Nina Dobrev). Up next are six book adaptations: the NY Times bestseller Can You Keep a Secret?, starring Alexandra Daddario; the NY Times bestseller The Language of Flowers, directed by Michael Mayer and starring Kiersey Clemons; NY Times bestselling supernatural romance franchise Hush, Hush, directed by Kellie Cyrus; The Pecan Man, directed by So Yong Kim; 2018 USA Today bestseller The Hating Game, starring Lucy Hale; and Marie Lu’s #1 NY Times bestselling blockbuster sci-fi trilogy Legend.
