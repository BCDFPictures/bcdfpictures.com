    Website Visitors

    Like most website operators, BCDF Pictures collects non-personally identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. BCDF Pictures’ purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how BCDF Pictures’ visitors use its website. From time to time, BCDF Pictures may release non-personally identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.


    Protection of Certain Personally Identifying Information

    BCDF Pictures discloses potentially personally identifying and personally identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on BCDF Pictures’ behalf or to provide services available at BCDF Pictures’ website and (ii) have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using BCDF Pictures’ website, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. BCDF Pictures will not rent or sell potentially personally identifying and personally identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations, as described above, BCDF Pictures discloses potentially personally identifying and personally identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order, or other governmental request or when BCDF Pictures believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of BCDF Pictures, third parties, or the public at large. If you are a registered user of the BCDF Pictures website and have supplied your email address, BCDF Pictures may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new projects, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with BCDF Pictures and our projects. If you send us a request (for example, via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. BCDF Pictures takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally identifying and personally identifying information.


    Cookies

    A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and what the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. BCDF Pictures uses cookies to help BCDF Pictures identify and track visitors, their usage of the BCDF Pictures website, and their website access preferences. BCDF Pictures visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using BCDF Pictures’ website, with the drawback that certain features of BCDF Pictures’ website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur and that any acquirer of BCDF Pictures may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
